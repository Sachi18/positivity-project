3 Ingredient Bars

A personal favorite, Everything Bar:

You'll need:

  • 1 cup of puffed rice or cereal (Cornflakes recommended)
  • 1/4 cup any nut butter
  • 1/8 crushed nuts(optional but recommended)

Take the cereal and crush it until desired sizes. Add in the nut butter gradually until you have a doughy consistancy (It may be a little sticky). If you want a crunchy texture add in some crushed nuts. Grease a pan and spoon the mixture into it. Chill for at least 30 minutes(depends on how sticky your mixture is). Then, you can drizzle some melted chocolate on top, cut it into bars and enjoy!

4. Smile.

Smiling. Seems pretty simple doesn't it? Smile at literaly everyone (not in a creepy way though). You'd be surprised at how big an impact a small gesture can make.
